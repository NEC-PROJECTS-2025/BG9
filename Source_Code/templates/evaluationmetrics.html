Evaluation Metrics

  • Accuracy: 85% - Percentage of correct predictions made by the model.
  • Precision: 0.90 - Measures how many positive predictions were correct.
  • Recall: 0.88 - Measures how many actual positive cases were correctly identified.
  • F1-Score: 0.89 - Harmonic mean of precision and recall.
  • Confusion Matrix: Shows the breakdown of predicted vs. actual values.
  • AUC-ROC: 0.92 - Measures the trade-off between true positive rate and false positive rate.
